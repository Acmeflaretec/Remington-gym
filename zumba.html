Zumba

ZUMBA DANCE CLASSES
Zumba is a Latin-inspired cardio dance workout class suitable for all ages and fitness levels. It improves your cardio, coordination and overall physical fitness. In this class, get ready to dance to a variety of music from Latin to African beats to hip-hop and pop!. Dancing to the beats of salsa, flamenco, reggaeton and hip hop music feels more like a dance party than a workout, which is exactly what makes Zumba so popular. The Latin-inspired dance workout is one of our most popular group exercise classes. The high-energy classes are set to upbeat music and feature structured choreography. You don’t need to be a great dancer to feel welcome in our Zumba class; just join the party, the classes emphasize moving to the music and having a good time, no rhythm required. Working up a sweat in the 60-minute classes burns an average of 369 calories — more than cardio kick-boxing or step aerobics. You’ll get a great cardio workout that melts fat, strengthens your core, and improves flexibility.

What Should I Know About Zumba?

Intensity:

On the intensity side, our Zumba class is an interval workout. The classes move between reasonable high- and low-intensity dance moves designed to get your heart rate up and boost cardio endurance.

Core:

Yes. Many of the dance steps used in the routines emphasize the hips and midsection to help strengthen the core.

Arms:

Yes, We do target the arms to help strengthen and tone the arms and enhance to flexibility around the shoulder area.

Legs:

Yes. The jumps and lunges that are parts of the choreographed movements help work the quads and hamstrings.

Glutes:

Yes. You’ll feel the burn in your buns while you move to the beat.

Back:

No. Though the workout involves your whole body, it’s not focused on your back muscles.

Flexibility:

Yes. The dance moves were designed to enhance flexibility.

Aerobic:

Yes. The high-and low-intensity intervals make Zumba an excellent cardio workout.

Strength:

Yes. Our Zumba workout emphasizes strengthening the core, the arms, legs, and glutes.

Good for beginners:

Yes. Our Zumba emphasizes moving to the music and having fun regardless of your fitness level.

Equipment required:

None, except for your sneakers.
